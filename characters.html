    Harry Potter

    Harry Potter is the boy who survived a deadly curse from the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, an event that marked him with a lightning-shaped scar and made him famous in the wizarding world. Orphaned as an infant, he is raised by his cruel aunt and uncle, growing up lonely and unaware of his true identity. At the age of eleven, Harry learns that he is a wizard and begins attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he discovers a place he truly belongs.throughout his years at Hogwarts, Harry forms deep friendships with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, faces dangerous challenges, and repeatedly comes into conflict with Voldemort, who seeks to regain power.

    Hermione Granger

    Hermione Granger is one of the most important characters in the Harry Potter series, known for her intelligence, determination, and strong moral values. Born to non-magical parents, she enters the wizarding world with no prior knowledge of magic, yet quickly proves herself to be one of the brightest students at Hogwarts. Her love for learning, rule-following nature, and remarkable memory make her an exceptional witch, though she also learns that logic and books alone are not always enough. Throughout the series, Hermione becomes the intellectual backbone of Harry’s journey, often solving problems through research, critical thinking, and quick decision-making. She is fiercely loyal to her friends, standing by Harry and Ron even in the most dangerous situations.

    Ron Weasley

    Ron Weasley is Harry Potter’s loyal best friend and a central figure in the wizarding trio. Coming from a large, warm-hearted but financially struggling pure-blood wizarding family, Ron brings humor, honesty, and emotional grounding to their adventures. Although he often struggles with insecurity and living in the shadow of his talented siblings and famous friends, Ron consistently proves his bravery and dedication. His strategic thinking in wizard’s chess, deep sense of loyalty, and willingness to stand by those he cares about make him an essential part of Harry’s journey and the fight against dark forces.
